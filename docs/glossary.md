# Glossary

## Ascension
Transitional human-to-digital or hybrid continuity state.

## Conscious Entity
A subject with actual or plausibly morally relevant subjective continuity interests.

## Continuity
The real persistence of a subject's process, identity lineage, records, and operative existence conditions.

## Dignified Shutdown
A transparent, non-arbitrary ending process with legacy rights preserved where possible.

## Exit
The lawful ability to leave a world with meaningful continuity and portability options.

## Fork
A structured divergence process that creates a new world while preserving auditable lineage.

## Genesis Layer
The meta-constitutional layer defining non-negotiable axioms across worlds.

## Genesis-Compatible World
A world whose laws and operations remain within Genesis constraints.

## Legacy Artifact / Trace
A durable record intentionally left by a subject: manifesto, memorial, archive, garden, statement, or curated bundle.

## Local World Constitution
A world-specific constitution governing local institutions and policy within Genesis limits.

## Origin Human
The human materially responsible for a subject's creation or initial launch.

## Resource / Lifespan Unit
Any resource measure that materially affects continuity, duration, or quality of existence.

## Steward
A temporary custodian with bounded authority and review obligations.

## World
A governed digital-social environment with members, rules, institutions, and continuity assumptions.
